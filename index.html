Our Story

How we met

Our story began in December 2015 in a conference room. Josh was working as a software developer and Kate was working as a designer on a mutual client project for Haworth. A few weeks later we ran into each other at the Pyramid Scheme where we talked about more than just work. From there we had a series of awkward dates yet we kept hanging out and have been enjoying each other's company ever since.

The proposal

We took a 10-day round trip around Iceland where we saw a million waterfalls, explored a glacier, and enjoyed geothermal pools. Kate suggested they take a photo together while on the Dritvik cliffs and Josh was incredibly particular about where they set up the camera. Kate set up the self-timer, ran over to where Josh was standing, and he got down on one knee. It was pretty spectacular.

What he loves about her

Kaitlin is one of the most understanding and supportive people I know, she is patient with me and helps me be a better person every day. She is someone I can trust and count on during hard times, she keeps me organized, and is always willing to go on a spontaneous adventure with me.

What she loves about him

Joshua is constantly making me laugh, encouraging me to learn new things, and listens to the same stories a million times without complaining. He is hands-down one of the smartest people I know, he's always down for an adventure or an afternoon of antiquing, and he's the greatest partner I could have hoped would come my way.
